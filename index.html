Tinovation + Cupertino High School






12.3.2016

about

8 hours. 100 high-schoolers. Free pizza. Over the course of the hackathon, students from all over the Fremont Union High School district filled the lower and upper levels of our school cafeteria and brought with them a solid conviction to learn and build great things! Between raffles, workshops, and free food, teams of up to 5 attendees worked hard to create a web-based, iOS, Android, or hardware hack of their choice. At the end of the day, all of the teams presented their work to a diverse judging panel in the form of a presentation and live demonstration. The top three teams , as decided by the judges, walked away with some neat prizes, but every attendee walked away with an even better prize: invaluable experience!
